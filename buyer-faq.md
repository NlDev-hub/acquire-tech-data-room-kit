# Buyer FAQ Starter — Technical Diligence

Status: public validation template. No payment, real-data, legal/tax/M&A, compliance/security, or outcome claims authorized.

## Safe answer rule

Every answer should be one of:
- Answered with evidence path;
- Unknown and needs verification;
- Not applicable with reason.

Do not invent claims. Do not imply certification, compliance, security assurance, sale readiness, valuation impact, or ROI.

## Questions

1. What are the main product components?
2. Where is production hosted?
3. Who has admin access?
4. How are deployments made and rolled back?
5. Which third-party APIs are critical?
6. Are dependency lockfiles present?
7. Is there an SBOM or dependency inventory?
8. Are known vulnerabilities tracked?
9. Is there a backup and restore process?
10. Are incidents/security issues logged?
11. What technical debt should a buyer know before diligence?
12. What requires founder knowledge transfer?

## Blocked use

Do not fill this FAQ with real company data or use it in a transaction, payment flow, or diligence process without a separate high-risk approval.
